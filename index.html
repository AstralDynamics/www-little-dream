Tucked away close to the cooling lake shores of Kandalama wewa, Little Dream guest house is a little gem which has been a refuge for tourists for over 20 years.

Surrounded by an eight acre eco-friendly garden, the property looks onto a jungle stream — a paradise for bird watchers and nature lovers. Little dream is the perfect tranquil setting to relax and forget the bustle of city life. Little dream is a wonderful place to stay at any time of the year. If the tourist trail is not for you you can spend your days here, surrounded by lush green countryside watching the clouds go by, or taking walks along the lake shore and exloring the river and local countryside. Alternatively, were also conveniently located close to Sigiria, Dambulla rock temple and many other attractions and just 15 minutes from Dambulla. and of course sampling our home cooked delicious Sri Lankan food.

Rooms

We have beautiful AC and Non-AC room with en suite bathrooms and hot water showers. The rooms have polished tiles and always kept very clean. Enjoy a quiet night’s sleep on fine quality beds away from the hustle and bustle of the towns.

Restaurant

In our spacious restaurant we serve highest quality veg and non-veg Sri Lanka meals made from the finest local ingredients. Our food is always very popular with our guests, and many come back for it especially. Breakfast is served fresh whenever you’re ready.
